Compute:: KDE

KDE estimates the joint probability density function (,) for the data.
When you evaluate KDE on a grid of X and Y values, you get a matrix where each element represents the joint density at that specific (X, Y) point.
density_values: This is the joint density estimate ( , ) , computed by evaluating the KDE on the entire grid of (Xgrid, Ygrid) points.
After normalization, each column represents a probability distribution over Y, given a fixed X:
normalized_density_values represents the entire conditional distribution matrix ( ) over all evaluated X values.
